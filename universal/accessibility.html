Accessibility Policy

We have chosen an accessible web design company.

Websites should be accessible to every single one of us.

Updated: February 09, 2024

This business has chosen us, GlitchWizard Solutions, as their website designer. We understand that technology is evolving, and that our role as an accessibility webmaster is ongoing, on behalf of this business, and all businesses that choose our services.

At GlitchWizard Solutions, our mission is to make our corner of the World Wide Web, an inclusive and welcoming space for everyone.

Our Commitment to Accessibility:

Universal Access: We are dedicated to designing and developing websites that provide universal access, allowing all visitors, to seamlessly browse and engage with content.

Compliance: Our websites adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA standards to the best of our ability. We remain committed to staying informed about the latest accessibility guidelines and enhancements.

Assistive Technologies: Our websites are designed to be compatible with assistive technologies such as screen readers, voice recognition software, and alternative input devices.

Color Contrast: We pay careful attention to color choices, ensuring that text and information are distinguishable for individuals with color blindness.

Font Size: We employ legible and resizable fonts to accommodate varying visual abilities. Text remains clear and easily readable, even when enlarged.
